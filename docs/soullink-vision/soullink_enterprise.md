# Soullink Enterprise (B2B Dashboard)

## Concept

A wellness platform for corporations that uses Vedic wisdom and AI to improve team harmony, reduce burnout, and foster a "Dharmic Work Culture."

## Key Features

1.  **Team Astrology:**
    - HR managers can see "Harmony Heatmaps" for their teams.
    - Identifies potential personality clashes and suggests "Communication Bridges."
2.  **Corporate Sadhana:**
    - Company-wide meditation challenges.
    - Private "Soul Circles" for employees to support each other.
3.  **Burnout Analytics:**
    - Aggregated, anonymous data on team "Karmic Stress" levels.
    - AI-suggested interventions (e.g., "The team is high in Rajas; suggest a 10-minute cooling meditation").

## Monetization

- **SaaS Model:** Per-employee monthly subscription.
- **Custom Workshops:** Access to Soullink's expert network for leadership training.
