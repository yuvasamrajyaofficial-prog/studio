# Go-To-Market & Viral Marketing Strategy

## 1. Phase 1: The "Inner Circle" (Beta Launch)

- **Target:** Spiritual influencers, yoga instructors, and mental health advocates.
- **Action:** Invite-only beta access to build a core community of "Sattvic" users.

## 2. Phase 2: The "Viral Hook" (Dream Decoder)

- **Target:** Gen Z and Millennials on TikTok/Instagram.
- **Action:** Shareable "Dream Cards" generated by the AI. "What does your soul want to tell you?"
- **Campaign:** #SoullinkDreams challenge.

## 3. Phase 3: The "Corporate Wedge" (Enterprise)

- **Target:** HR Directors in tech hubs (Bangalore, San Francisco, London).
- **Action:** Free "Team Harmony" audits for selected companies.
- **Partnership:** Collaborate with employee wellness platforms.

## 4. Phase 4: Global Expansion

- **Target:** International markets interested in "Eastern Wisdom."
- **Action:** Localized versions in Spanish, Japanese, and German.
- **Content:** Partnerships with global spiritual leaders and psychologists.

## 5. Retention & Growth

- **Karmic Referrals:** Users earn GKP for inviting friends who complete their first Sadhana.
- **Daily Rituals:** Push notifications tied to "Cosmic Weather" to ensure daily app opens.
