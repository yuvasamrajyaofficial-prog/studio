# Expert Network Onboarding

## 1. The "Council of Sages"

- **Scholars:** Verified experts in Sanskrit, Vedas, and Puranas.
- **Psychologists:** Licensed professionals specializing in transpersonal or depth psychology.
- **Mentors:** Experienced practitioners with a high "Karmic Signature" within the app.

## 2. Onboarding Process

- **Verification:** Background checks on academic credentials or professional licenses.
- **Alignment:** A short course on Soullink's philosophy and AI ethics.
- **Profile Creation:** A specialized "Expert Soul ID" that highlights their area of mastery.

## 3. Roles & Responsibilities

- **Content Review:** Fact-checking AI-generated summaries and translations.
- **Satsang Hosting:** Leading live audio/video sessions in Soul Circles.
- **Crisis Support:** Providing human-level intervention when escalated by the AI.

## 4. Incentives

- **Revenue Share:** A percentage of subscription revenue for active contributors.
- **Reputation:** Earning "Maha-Karma" points and digital badges.
- **Impact:** Reaching a global audience with their wisdom.
