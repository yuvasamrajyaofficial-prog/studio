# App Sitemap & Navigation Flow

## 1. High-Level Sitemap

- **Onboarding:** Soul ID Creation (Astrology + Psych Quiz).
- **Home (The Cosmic Hub):**
  - 3D Cosmic Flow Engine (Background/Interactive).
  - Daily Karma Meter & Soul Signature.
  - Quick Access: AI Guru, Library, Soul Circles.
- **The Guru (Voice/Chat):**
  - Full-screen immersive voice interface.
  - Chat history & Wisdom bookmarks.
- **The Living Library:**
  - Categorized Scripture Tree.
  - Reading View (Flipbook/Scroll).
  - Audio/Binaural Player.
- **Soul Circles (Social):**
  - Circle Discovery.
  - Active Study Groups.
  - Karmic Gifting.
- **Profile/Soul ID:**
  - Astrological Birth Chart.
  - Karmic Analytics Dashboard.
  - Settings & Privacy.
- **Enterprise (B2B Only):**
  - Team Harmony Dashboard.
  - Corporate Sadhana Management.

## 2. Navigation Flow

- **Primary Navigation:** Bottom bar with 5 icons (Home, Library, Guru, Circles, Profile).
- **Gestures:**
  - **Swipe Up from Home:** Open the AI Guru (Quick Chat).
  - **Pinch to Zoom on Home:** Enter/Exit the 3D Cosmic Flow Engine.
  - **Double Tap on Scripture:** Bookmark or "Aha!" moment.
- **Transitions:** Fluid, "cosmic" transitions (fades, star-field zooms) to maintain the "Digital Temple" aesthetic.
