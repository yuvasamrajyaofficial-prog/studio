# Legal & Compliance Framework

## 1. Business Structure

- **Entity:** Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) or Private Limited Company (to facilitate venture capital and grants).
- **Intellectual Property (IP):** All AI models, algorithms (Soul ID), and custom scripture translations owned by the entity.

## 2. Data Privacy & Security

- **GDPR/CCPA Compliance:** Essential for global expansion.
- **HIPAA Compliance:** Necessary if the app is marketed as a mental health tool in the US.
- **Encryption:** End-to-end encryption for all user-AI conversations.
- **Anonymization:** Aggregated data for Enterprise dashboards must be fully anonymized.

## 3. AI Ethics & Content

- **Disclaimer:** Clear messaging that the AI Guru is a spiritual guide, not a medical professional.
- **Copyright:** Ensuring all scripture translations and media are either in the public domain or properly licensed.
- **Crisis Protocol:** Legal indemnity through robust safety guardrails and clear terms of service.

## 4. Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

- Transparent disclosure on how data is used to personalize the experience.
- Easy "Right to be Forgotten" (one-click account and data deletion).
