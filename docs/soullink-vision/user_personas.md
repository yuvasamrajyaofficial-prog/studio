# Soullink User Personas

## 1. The Seeker (Aarav, 24)

- **Background:** Tech professional in Bangalore, feeling burnt out and "spiritually thirsty."
- **Pain Point:** Finds traditional religion too ritualistic but modern meditation apps too shallow.
- **Goal:** Wants to understand the "Why" behind existence and find practical tools for daily stress.
- **Soullink Usage:** Uses the **AI Guru** for late-night philosophical chats and the **Living Library** to read the Gita with AI explanations.

## 2. The Corporate (Sarah, 42)

- **Background:** HR Director in a Fortune 500 company.
- **Pain Point:** High team turnover and "clashing personalities" in high-pressure environments.
- **Goal:** Wants a data-driven way to improve team harmony and employee well-being.
- **Soullink Usage:** Uses **Soullink Enterprise** for "Corporate Soul-Matching" to build balanced teams and offers the **Binaural Scripture Chanting** as a wellness benefit.

## 3. The Healer (Priya, 35)

- **Background:** Yoga instructor and wellness coach.
- **Pain Point:** Struggles to explain the deep metaphysical concepts of the Vedas to her students.
- **Goal:** Wants a visual and interactive way to teach the "Cosmic Flow" of energy and time.
- **Soullink Usage:** Uses the **3D Cosmic Engine** to visualize planetary alignments and the **AR Temple** to create immersive meditation spaces for her classes.

## 4. [NEW] The Elder (Dr. Gupta, 68)

- **Background:** Retired professor, deeply knowledgeable but lonely.
- **Pain Point:** Wants to share wisdom but feels disconnected from the digital age.
- **Goal:** To find a community of like-minded souls and leave a "spiritual legacy."
- **Soullink Usage:** Earns high **Good Karma Points** by mentoring others in **Soul Circles** and recording audio commentaries for the library.
