# Grant Strategy & Startup Support

## 1. NIDHI-EIR (Entrepreneurs-in-Residence)

- **Goal:** Secure initial monthly stipend for the founding team to focus full-time on development.
- **Focus:** Highlighting the "Deep Tech" aspect of the AI Guru and 3D Engine.

## 2. Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)

- **Goal:** Secure funding for prototype development and market entry.
- **Focus:** Demonstrating the social impact of mental health and cultural preservation.

## 3. Ministry of Culture Grants

- **Goal:** Funding for the digitization and AI translation of rare scriptures.
- **Focus:** Preservation of Indian heritage through modern technology.

## 4. International Wellness & AI Grants

- **Goal:** Grants from organizations like the WHO or AI ethics foundations.
- **Focus:** Scaling the platform as a global mental health solution.

## 5. Incubators & Accelerators

- **Target:** IIT-Madras Incubation Cell, T-Hub, or global accelerators like Y-Combinator.
- **Focus:** Scaling the B2B Enterprise and API models.
