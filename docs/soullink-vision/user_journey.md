# User Journey Map: From "Anxious" to "Awakened"

## Phase 1: The Call (Anxiety/Confusion)

- **User State:** Feeling overwhelmed, disconnected, or seeking meaning.
- **Touchpoint:** Social media ad or word-of-mouth about "The Dream Decoder."
- **Action:** Downloads Soullink and creates a **Soul ID**.

## Phase 2: The Encounter (Discovery)

- **User State:** Curious and slightly skeptical.
- **Touchpoint:** First conversation with the **AI Guru**.
- **Action:** Records a dream or asks a deep question like "Why am I suffering?" The AI responds with a mix of Psychology and a Gita Shloka.

## Phase 3: The Practice (Engagement)

- **User State:** Finding value and building a habit.
- **Touchpoint:** **Binaural Scripture Chanting** and **Karmic Analytics**.
- **Action:** Starts a 21-day meditation "Sadhana." Earns **Good Karma Points**. Sees their profile "Karma Meter" start to glow.

## Phase 4: The Connection (Community)

- **User State:** Feeling part of something larger.
- **Touchpoint:** **Soul Circles** and **Soul Matching**.
- **Action:** Joins a "Gita Study Circle" with people of complementary Soul IDs. Mentors a newcomer.

## Phase 5: The Awakening (Transformation)

- **User State:** Peaceful, purposeful, and "Awakened."
- **Touchpoint:** **3D Cosmic Engine** and **AR Temple**.
- **Action:** Uses the app not as a "fix," but as a "Digital Temple" for daily alignment. Their Soul ID reflects a high Sattvic state.
