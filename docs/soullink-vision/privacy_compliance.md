# Data Privacy & Compliance (HIPAA/GDPR)

## 1. Data Sovereignty

- **User Ownership:** Users own their data, including their Soul ID and chat history.
- **Portability:** Users can export their spiritual journey data at any time.

## 2. Security Standards

- **Encryption:** AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit.
- **Anonymization:** Personal Identifiable Information (PII) is separated from spiritual/psychological data.
- **Zero-Knowledge Architecture:** For highly sensitive features like the Dream Decoder, where even Soullink staff cannot access the raw audio/text.

## 3. Regulatory Compliance

- **GDPR (Europe):** Full compliance with "Right to Access" and "Right to Erasure."
- **HIPAA (US):** Implementing BAA (Business Associate Agreements) with cloud providers to ensure health data security.
- **DPDP (India):** Adhering to the Digital Personal Data Protection Act.

## 4. Transparency

- Clear, non-legalese privacy policy explaining exactly how the AI uses user data to "learn" and personalize the experience.
